My Daily Routine

  1. 6:30 AM - 7:30 AM

    Morning Reset
    Wake up, freshen up, make coffee, quick scroll (unfortunately), get ready for the day. Breakfast is optional.

    Assist in SRA class, absorbing knowledge, taking notes, and trying to stay ahead. Only two one-and-half hour breaks within the day for lunch and short break.

    Recuperation
    Dinner, homework, consolidation of today's reminders and events, scroll or game. And finally, sleep.
